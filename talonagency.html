Background Checks

Project Overview:

Talon Agency LLC was work as a senior project to help private investigators handle more remote clients by developing a web application to streamline the process of approving clients, retrieving background check requests, secure payment methods, and distribution of confidential reports.

View it live

The Design:

I was given the responsiblity of branding, designing, implementing, and testing the front end. The original owner of the business requested that the theme resemble a “patriotic professionalism”, which to me meant a clean, modern user interface combined with a modern red, white, and blue color scheme.

I chose using animated illustrations with bodies of text on the landing page to provide clear instructions on who Talon Agency is, what it does, and how to use it. Finally, I took all these designs from paper and combined them into a styling guiltiness PDF for my teammates to use as they developed new pages and features of the site.

The Development:

My intention for this design, and all my designs, was to do all the design work upfront so that the development cycle goes smoothly and can move into testing faster. Using bootstrap to build it responsively, I created SVG illustrations and animated them using CSS, Javascript, and JQuery. I used browser tools while implementing to make sure the site looked good no matter what device accessed it, and that it met accessibility standards.

Due to the small size of our team, I contributed to the backend as well during many of our sprints. I helped build associations and permissions for the Ruby backend, authentication using Auth0, and e-commerce payments using Stripe API. We used the methodology of behavior driven development, and our team maintained an automated test coverage of 94.53%.

Skills and Thoughts:

The skills I learned for this project I would say were mostly working on an agile development team to produce a professional grade product. Learning how to schedule meetings, plan sprints, deliver on due dates, commit and merge code, and simply interact with a team of developers were all things this project helped me grow in. Additionally, it helped open me up to the balance of art and science found in front end development.

Due to time constraints on the project, our minimum viable product took priority over some things I would have liked to properly implement. If I knew then what I know now, I would have liked to make the site fit a mobile display more comfortably / naturally and made the javascript animations cleaner.

Overall, this project was incredibly engaging, rewarding, and all around an amazing learning experience!
