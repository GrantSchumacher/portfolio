Developer and Designer
Grant Schumacher

I’ve has always had a passion for creativity. Drawing, designing, planning, and engineering since I was young. During my time studying Computer Science at MSU Denver, I found I could combine my creativity with code to create beautiful user experiences, and it felt like the perfect place to express myself and have a real impact on people.

I’m a self starter, hard worker, and all around a down to earth person who loves being outside. My hobbies are what give me life, so I founded a coffee roasting business during college that I branded and built an e-commerce site for orders with, which was a huge learning experience. During that time I worked as an intern at Agency Technology building an API to provide automated software solutions for the travel industry, where I learned what working in a professional environment was like and how important team organization is.

Before graduating in May of 2021, I had the opportunity to work on an amazing team of students to develop Talon Agency, a site for digital, comprehensive background checks. Which served as our senior project and a representation of my frontend skills and how they are progressing daily.

Now that leaves me off where I am today, looking to learn, grow, and create in a way that impacts people positively. If you want to help me on this journey please contact me!
