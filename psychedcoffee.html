Branding and Selling
Delicous Coffee

Project Overview:

Psyched Coffee Roasters was a business I co-founded and branded during college to connect people around the world through selling delicous, fresh roasted coffee. We operated online, at events, and at farmers markets. I designed and created the energetic brand using explosive colors. As a co-founder, I held many roles, but the most notable was designing all the branding and building an e-commerce site to manage our online sales using Shopify.

See the brand

The Design:

With name like Psyched, the branding came naturally. Our brand was not going to be another black and white, hipster coffee roastery, it needed to be explosive, colorful, and relatable. So our brand and marketing was built around explosions of color and high energy photographs, to break norms in the coffee world and show people that anyone can have fun with coffee!

The Development:

The development of Psyched coffee was a lot of work and a great learning experience. It involved finding farmers from around the world, connecting with coffee importers, purchasing and operating a commercial grade roaster, building an e-commerce site, growing a social media following, and attending events and markets to sell our product. My knowledge was in sourcing and roasting coffee as well as branding, while my business partner had a background in business and accounting, so together we found a well balanced team.

I developed the e-commerce site using Shopify, which is no longer live, but handled our online orders and held information about our company and why we existed.

Final Thoughts:

The intention of founding this business was for me to gain skills in building a brand and roasting delicious coffee, and for my partner to build skills in business and accounting, which we both accomplished. Our plan before even starting was not to turn this into a large business, but to have fun, learn, break-even, and operate for over 6 months, which we completely achieved. We later closed the business as planned and moved on to new challenges, but still have close friends and community because of it!
